Common Questions

Does VirtueKeeper work offline?

Yes. You can check in, view your history, earn points, and unlock milestones without an internet connection. Changes sync the next time you go online.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Your Data → Export. You can export as CSV or JSON and share via the system share sheet.

Is there a cost?

No. VirtueKeeper is free. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscription.

Privacy concerns?

See the Privacy Policy for details on what data is collected and how it is used. VirtueKeeper does not sell or share personal data.
